Features

Everything you need to build your sound

From discovery to collaboration, Beatpax gives producers the tools to create, share, and grow.

Explore

Browse a growing library of beats and sound pax across every genre. Filter by BPM, key, and mood to find your perfect sound.

Curate

Build curated collections from any beats on the platform. Organize your favorites and share playlists with collaborators.

Stems

Upload and share individual stem tracks for real collaboration. Let others build on your work or remix with full stems access.

Upload

Share your beats with the world. Upload tracks, set pricing in tokens, and start building your producer profile.

Buy and sell beats with tokens. A simple, transparent marketplace that puts creators first and keeps things fair.
